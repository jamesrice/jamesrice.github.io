Bio

Hi! I'm James Rice

Raised on an Idaho farm. My first startup in 1995 was Mediaphex, one of the first and finest web agencies in the world. Now living in Portland, Oregon. Co-Founder and CEO of Fiction Tribe, a multidisciplinary creative studio. My next startup, Lagoon, officially launched in 2023. I currently serve as Chairman of Tribe Global, an international creative network. I love to make.
