Background:
Raised in the mile high tundra of southern Idaho, I was introduced to branding,
loud engines on fast metal, working the earth and respecting predatory wolves
- all before escaping my cloth diapers. Without a television antenna,
my only access to media was through business reply cards in magazines,
resulting in my formative years being defined by a weekly stuffed mailbox of direct mail,
Sharper Image and LL Bean catalogs. My parents were perplexed, but I was immersing
myself in marketing and brand, moving from the "hot iron on flesh" approach to the
"damn, this is cool" method. Then they bought me an Apple ]I[ computer. I became a
GenXer living on a farm playing Zork and milking cows. I did indeed buck bales of hay,
but only with hydraulic powered heavy equipment. So I wasn't a strapping lad, but went
off to college with a nerd scholarship, made money by building computers and upgrading
video cards to display 64 colors at a time for my peers - yet wasn't ingenious enough to
turn that effort into Dell. I went to graduate school for critical theory and that
summer a web browser called CeIlo appeared. I was hooked, so I started a business to
make HoTMetaL pages because there wasn't anywhere that did that. It was awesome. And then
Netscape appeared and HOLY SHIT LIFE CHANGED. I never finished my graduate thesis. I
didn't sleep much, as everything I wanted to build had to be created. Somewhere along
the way I transitioned from maker to leader. Looking back, years later, I've been an
entrepreneur that builds digital creative firms to fulfill my love of brand and technology,
and that gives clients the best digital transformation results. I've been fortunate to know
both inspiring creative folks and the vapid narcissistic ones throughout my career. Those
interactions gave rise to my design of business to make sure it works for those who work for it.

Bio

Raised on an Idaho farm. My first startup in 1995 was Mediaphex, one of the first and finest web agencies in the world. Now living in Portland, Oregon. Co-Founder and CEO of Fiction Tribe, a multidisciplinary creative studio. My next startup, Lagoon, officially launched in 2023. I currently serve as Chairman of Tribe Global, an international creative network.

I love to make and explore the outdoors.
